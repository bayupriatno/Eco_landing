Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about EcoGreen

What is EcoGreen (EGR)?

EcoGreen is a blockchain-based token that rewards users for environmental actions like tree planting, recycling, and carbon footprint reduction. It combines Web3 technology with real-world environmental impact.

How do I join the whitelist?

Simply fill out the whitelist form above with your details. Whitelist members get early access to the presale, bonus tokens, and exclusive community benefits.

When is the presale?

The presale is scheduled for Q4 2025. Whitelist members will receive priority access and exclusive pricing before the public sale.

How can I earn EGR tokens?

You can earn EGR tokens by participating in verified environmental actions such as tree planting, recycling, using renewable energy, and reducing your carbon footprint through our platform.

Is there a minimum investment?

Minimum investment details will be announced closer to the presale date. Whitelist members will receive this information first.

How is EcoGreen different from other crypto projects?

EcoGreen focuses specifically on environmental impact and sustainability. Unlike other tokens, EGR rewards real-world green actions and partners with environmental NGOs to create tangible positive change.
